Ricochet is a multiplayer Half-Life mod by Valve in 2000. It's simple. Stand on arrows aiming at a platform and kill the other players. Simple as that. The controls are exactly the same as other Valve games, except the fact CTRL doesn't let you crouch this time.

Other than that, the gameplay is almost the same as every 90s-early 2000s Valve game. The framerate again is amazing. The map is too small to be honest. I'd honestly play it more if there were more people playing the game. If the maps were more creative I'd also play even more than I already do. I'd love it if there were maps that had walls and stuff so you can't see where the other players are, making the game harder.

The character design is kind of eh. The powerups are actually really good, I love how you can combine more than 1.

The sound design here is awful. The screaming sound when you fall off of one of those areas gives me a headache. It's annoying and it hurts my ears.

This game was probably rushed but I still think it's a really fun game. I hope the game gets more active one of these days. I got it yesterday for 49 cents but the game at full price is $4.99.

I don't recommend the game if you don't want to waste money on a game nobody plays. I recommend it if you find an active server. It's quite fun.

Final score: 60%

As I said, it was rushed, sound design is awful, character design wasn't that great, nobody plays it too much, maps aren't creative, and that's all I didn't like about this game. I think it had a potential and it still is a really fun game. If you want to buy it though, I recommend buying it during a sale or something. I got it for 49 cents which is a pretty cheap price. So yeah, I don't recommend it...but I do if you happen upon a server. So, I'm divisive with this one... Thanks for reading the review!
